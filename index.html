About Me

I am a PhD-qualified Spatial Data Scientist with an expertise that spans from managing spatial databases and developing scalable data pipelines to designing innovative machine learning solutions. My work fuses advanced geospatial analytics with state-of-the-art AI techniques, ensuring that even the most complex data challenges are met with creative and effective solutions.
